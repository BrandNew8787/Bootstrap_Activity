Tomo - Golden Retriever

A LITTLE ABOUT ME.

Hello there! My name is Tomo, and I am a golden retriever. I'm going to be 13 years old in a couple of weeks but I still act like puppy around everyone! During the day I love to sleep on my bed and go around asking for food from my owners. They generally give me carrots and ice cubes, but my favorite snack is the duck jerkey they give me during special occasions! They make me do tricks like sitting down, rolling over, shaking hands, and high fiving them and its really fun! Sometimes we go on walks around Lake Chabot or walk around on the streets of Castro Valley. It's really fun to go on those walks because I get to explore the world and see new people everytime! I love my life at home and always love everyone around me!
